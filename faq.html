How does Bill Splitter works?
It's is used to split the expenses among your friends. It keeps track of your expenses and also the money you owe to someone or someone else owing you money.
How do I use Bill Splitter?
First of all you need to sign up for an account, then you need to form a group of people with whom you wish to divide your expenses with. Then everyone needs to add their expenses and Bill Splitter will handle the rest once you click Split the Bill.
How do I let my friend know that what expenses are to be settled?
Once you click Split the Bill button, an email will be sent to each & every member of the group and apart from reminder the total amount to be paid will also be mentioned in the mail.
What are the modes of payment with which I can settle my expenses?
Payment gateway provides you various options through which the money can be sent or recieved including UPIs, Debit and Credit cards, Internet Banking etc.
What if the money is debited from the account but have'nt reached the reciever's account?
In that case you don't need to worry as the amount will be reflected in the reciever's account in a span of one or two days and if the transaction was'nt successful then money will be reflected in sender's account within 7 working days.
Is my privacy at risk?
No, not at all. We never share your data with anyone as user's privacy is our topmost concern and that's what made Bill Splitter a world class app. No one can access your data apart from you.
